    • If u have ever walked down your local street and noticed a giant pothhole in front of your path, or seen waterlogged streets in mild rains and ever wondered on how these problems often get overlooked, you are not alone! We at UdevCIty share your thought on how many everyday problems arent actively acted upon. We believe that the reason on why a problem is not solved is that our collective voices may not reach the concerned authorities. To tackle this issue we present a solution, UdevCity! A team of brilliant minds brought together with a common ambition-using the network to help solve problems and make sure that the problems faced by individuals everyday reach the concerned authorities. The name "UDevCity" comes from the simple expression on how "U" can play a part in the "dev"(development) of your "City". We are a private organisation, not linked to any government or other private organisation in any manner.

    Why?

    Our aim is "Development". There are many problems and issues faced regularly by people, that are usually viewed as small and arent well tended to. Hence, most people tend to ignore them altogether. UdevCity takes the initiative to make sure that issues are always reported to the concerned authorities. Eliminating all problems of all sorts and sizes is the only way to a healthy environment. As responsible members of this society, it is our duty to try and help the authorities in every way we can and achieve the coveted Smart City title.

    Bridging The Communication Gap

    The sole purpose of UdevCity is to bridge the communication gap between the user and the government. Those who want to raise a voice,grievance, or complain against an issue, can now use UdevCity to make the most systematic approach in such a scenario. With our innovative mechanism and vast networking system, reporting problems have never been easier. Just describe the problem in simple words(may also add visual references to it) and we will do the rest. Sounds easy right? Our team is actively working on sorting all types of reported problems and then making sure they reach the concerned authorities. If u have ever wondered "Who to report to", "How does the process work" or anything like that, with us on the scene u wont need to worry anymore. Your voice will always reach the concerned ears.

    How To Go About?

    1. Make a Udev City Account: It is crucial for all users to register onto the website for a better experience. The registration is safe and free of cost.
    2. Find a problem: Once registered, find a problem that u believe needs fixing.
    3. Use the features: Now, all users need to do is describe the problem and add some photos or videos for reference. Description doesnt need to be long and can be in simple words.
    4. Confirm the area : Once described, add the location to where the problem is which will help us pinpoint to who the nearest authority for that specific problem is
    5. Hit submit: Press the submit button and you're done! Once user submission has been verified, the concerned authorities will be alerted, and a response will be shared. Details on the status of the submission will be shared on registered user email.
